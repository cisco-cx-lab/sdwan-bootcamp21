3.3. Day 3 tasks

In Day 3 lab, you will configure central policy for route preference, arbitrary topology, and App-aware routing.

Central policy

3.3.1. Configure central route policy
* prefer local originated prefixes from DC1 (10.10.100.0/24) over DC1 TLOCs
* prefer local originated prefixes from DC2 (10.10.200.0/24) over DC2 TLOCs.
* DC2 will be used as backup path for DC1 prefix and DC1 will be used as backup path for DC2 prefix.

3.3.2. Configure central control policy
* build hub-spoke topology with DC1/DC2 as the hub sites and BR1/BR3 as spokes.
* block tunnels between DC1 and DC2 using central policy
* (optional) Create additional VPN across all sites and make it full mesh topology
