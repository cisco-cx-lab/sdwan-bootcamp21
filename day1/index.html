In Day 1 lab, you will change BR3 CSR1kv cEdge from vmanage mode to CLI mode. You will remove the pre-built device template for BR3-cEdge1. Then you will create new device template for BR3-cEdge1 based on the CLI configuration.

You need to take screenshot after each task is completed, and submit your screenshot to the instructor.
